Hello! My name is Darren Yau. I am a graduate student in the Department of Religion at Princeton University, working in the subfield of politics, religion, and ethics. My current interests focus on questions such as:

  • How have various kinds of religious commitments shaped the current landscape of moral and political thought;
  • What are the promises and perils of contemporary projects in political theology, Augustinian, liberatory, secular, or otherwise;
  • How should we think about various practices, such as forgiveness, reparations, and reconciliation, that are available to redress massive, structural historical injustices?
